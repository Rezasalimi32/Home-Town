Kermanshah is the capital of Kermanshah Province,
located 525 kilometres (326 miles) from Tehran in the western part of Iran.
According to the 2016 census, its population is 946,681 (2021 estimate 1,047,000).
A majority of the people of Kermanshah are bilingual in Southern Kurdish and Persian,
and the city is the largest Kurdish-speaking city in Iran.
Kermanshah has a moderate and mountainous climate.
